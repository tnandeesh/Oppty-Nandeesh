Classification of burns as per their severity.
Grade Criteria
Critical burns

• Partial thickness burns > 25% total body surface area (TBSA)

• Full thickness burns > 10%

• Burns of special sites like hands, face, and perineum

• Burns with associated injury or any preexisting illness and old age

• Electrical burns

• Burns complicated by respiratory tract involvement
Moderate burns

• Partial thickness burns 15–25% TBSA

• Full thickness burns 1–3% TBSA
